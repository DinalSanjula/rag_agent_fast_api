Increment Policy (Annual Salary Increment)

Policy basis
- An annual increment is granted to employees who are on the applicable salary scale, provided the employee’s work and conduct have been completely satisfactory during the previous 12-month period.

Increment review date / timing
- Annual increments are payable on 1st January of each year.
- 1st January is the common incremental review date for all employees.

New employees
- A new employee’s first increment is in January following his/her confirmation.

Process (Annual increments – procedure)
- The Unit Head evaluates the employee’s performance and submits the evaluation together with a recommendation for the annual increment (as per section “9.5.3 Procedure for Annual increments”).

Source
- Human Resource Policies & Procedures Manual (hr_manual.pdf), section 9.5 “Policy on Annual increments” (page labeled 45).
